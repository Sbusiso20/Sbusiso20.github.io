HOW TO LOG AN EMERGENCY

1. Logging In:

The driver opens the application or software designed for managing incidents. They are prompted to enter their credentials (username and password) on the login screen. After entering the correct credentials, they click on the "Login" or "Sign In" button.

2. Navigating to the Emergency Page:

Upon logging in, the driver is presented with a dashboard or a main menu. They locate and click on the "Emergency" or "Incidents" tab/button/icon in the navigation menu. This action directs them to the emergency incident management page.

3. Logging an Emergency Incident:

On the emergency incident management page, the driver sees a form or interface for logging new incidents. They fill in required fields such as incident type, location, description, and severity. Depending on the system, they may also be required to attach photos or documents related to the incident. After completing the form, they review the information for accuracy. Finally, they submit the form by clicking on a "Submit" or "Save" button.
