Route Information Guide

HOW TO LOCATE ROUTE INFORMATION

1. Logging In:

The driver opens the navigation application or software on their device. They are prompted to log in by entering their credentials (username and password) on the login screen. After entering the correct credentials, they click on the "Login" or "Sign In" button.

2. Accessing Routes:

Upon successful login, the driver is presented with a dashboard or a main menu. They locate and click on the "Routes" or "My Routes" tab/button/icon in the navigation menu. This action directs them to the page where they can view their saved or planned routes.

3. Locating Their Route Information:

On the routes page, the driver sees a list of saved or planned routes associated with their account. They scroll through the list or use a search/filter option to find the specific route they are interested in. Once they locate the desired route, they click on it to access detailed information.

4. Viewing Route Details:

Upon clicking on the route, the driver is taken to a page displaying detailed information about the route. This information may include the route name, start and end points, total distance, estimated travel time, waypoints, and any additional notes or instructions. The driver can review the route details to ensure it matches their intended journey.

5. Optional: Modifying Route (if needed):

If the driver needs to modify the route (e.g., add waypoints, avoid certain areas, or change the mode of transportation), they can do so using the provided options or tools on the route details page. After making any necessary modifications, they can save the changes or update the route accordingly.

6. Navigating Along the Route:

Once the driver is satisfied with the route information, they can initiate navigation by clicking on a "Start Navigation" or "Navigate" button. The navigation application then provides turn-by-turn directions, voice guidance, and real-time updates as the driver progresses along the route.
